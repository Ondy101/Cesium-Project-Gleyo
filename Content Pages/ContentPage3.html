Breaking Barriers: A Call to Action for Unity and Progress
Courtesy of Kelly on Pexels (n.d.)
Stay up to date with us.

"Uy Nigro!, Uy si Chinchong oh!" Racism is a negative habit that society simply cannot seem to break. Racism continues to infiltrate our communities, companies, and schools despite decades of progress, creating needless barriers on everyone. It is a systematic issue that impacts laws, regulations, and daily life, slowing progress and increasing divisions. It is not just a personal problem. This article explores the ways racism impacts communities and professions, demonstrating why addressing racism directly is not just long overdue but also essential for a suitable and successful society.

Racism is a widespread issue that affects many branches of society. In education, racial discrimination is linked to “lower school engagement and greater school disconnection” (Bottiani, et al. 2020). Similarly, racism leads to not only mental but also physical health issues, ultimately resulting in decreased workplace productivity (Gonzales, 2022). Individuals who experience this type of discrimination often seek refuge by keeping their silence against favored oppressors, and stories like these almost always go nowhere; it is an incessant problem that isn’t brought up loud enough by everyone.

Racism is an issue that continues to plague settings ranging from schools to companies and significantly disrupts their overall performance. However, it should not always end this way. It is time that we, as a society, strive together to put an end to racial discrimination. Ample intervention from teachers in educational settings, admins from both public and private companies, and even those in politics could go a long way for not only ourselves but also the world.
